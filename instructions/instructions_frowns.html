FROWNS

  • Frowning is when the eyebrows contract and move towards the nose.
  • Frowning STARTS from the frame when the eyebrows begin to move towards the nose. It ENDS at the frame when the eyebrows move back away from the nose so that WRINGKLES ARE NOT CLEARLY VISIBLE ANYMORE between the eyebrows.
  • For each frown, do your best to identify the EXACT starting frame and the ending frame.
  • Identify ALL instances of frowns.
  • If you are not quite sure whether it is a frown or not, DON'T annotate.
  • Have the sound on. It can help.
